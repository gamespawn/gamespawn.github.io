Welcome to Gamespawn!

Want to make games? Gamespawn can help! We host workshops, guest lectures, socials, and more.

Get started

Game Development For Everyone

Gamespawn is U.C. Riverside's game development club. Whether you're a programmer, artist, composer, or just a person that really loves games, Gamespawn has something to offer for everyone!

Workshops

Our student-led workshops cater towards all experience levels. Learn about 3D modeling, sound design, programming, and more.

Projects

Our members have made many brilliant games over the years. Join one of our active projects or share one of your own.

Socials

Sit back, relax, and hang out with the Gamespawn family at our fun socials. We'll bring games and snacks.

Meet the Pros

Industry professionals frequently visit Gamespawn to share their personal success stories and advice for aspiring game developers. Previously, we have invited developers from Obsidian Entertainment, Blizzard, and Turtle Rock Studios.
