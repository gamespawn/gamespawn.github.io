About

Who we are

Founded in 2014, Gamespawn @ University of California Riverside is a student organization that focuses on introducting students to game development. Our organization teaches students game design, art, and programming through competitions and guest speakers. We do our best not only to motivate and encourage students to learn about game development, but also to provide a professional and comfortable environment for engineers, programmers, designers, and artists.
