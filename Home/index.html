WELCOME TO GAMESPAWN AT UCR

We are computer programmers, technical artists, and level designers.
We work with everything game related. That includes attending awesome Game Jams, hosting workshops
that teach everything from basic graphics to advanced Unreal Engine tutorials, and having a great time!
Show up to a general meeting to learn more or attend any upcoming events that interest you.
