  • Resonance

    Team: Lorenzo Alamillo, Jeffrey Hsieh, Anjin Huang, Quynh Nguyen, Emmilio Segovia, David Zhang

    Email: dzhan008@ucr.edu

    Platform: PC

    This is an isometric puzzle game where you are a blind person lost in a cave. You have only your sense of sound to guide you. Little do you know that sounds can paint pictures...
    Controls: Use WASD to move. Aim your character with your mouse, and left click or press space to fire your sound waves.
    Instructions: This is a puzzle game where you can use echo waves to see across the map. By traversing the map, you will face obstacles you will need to overcome to get to the end of the level.

    Tags: 2D, completed

  • Cutie Hack

    Team: Montana Esguerra, Robyn Haley, Chris Pribyl, Carolina Rodriguez

    Email: cprib001@ucr.edu

    Platform: PC

    Quick! There's a bunch of computers you need to hack, and you are stressed under time. Type to your hearts content to unleash the inner hack within you!
    Controls: Arrow Keys to move. Use all your keys to hack!
    Instructions: Move up to a computer. Once you're in front of it, start mashing your keyboard to hack! If you stop at any point, the hack will fail and you will lose a life. You only get three lives, so be careful!
